## Cong Li (李聪)

![avatar]({{photo}} width=200px)

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the [Advanced Software Technologies (AST) Lab](https://ast.ethz.ch) at [ETH Zurich](https://ethz.ch/en), working with [Prof. Zhendong Su](https://people.inf.ethz.ch/suz).

Before joining ETH Zurich, I participated in the Ant Star Program and served as an Ant Star researcher at [Ant Group](https://www.antgroup.com/en), working with [Dr. Peng Di](https://pengd.github.io).

I received my Ph.D. from the [Institute of Computer Software (ICS)](https://ics.nju.edu.cn), [Nanjing University (NJU)](https://www.nju.edu.cn), advised by [Prof. Yanyan Jiang](https://ics.nju.edu.cn/people/yanyanjiang/index.html) and [Prof. Chang Xu](https://cs.nju.edu.cn/changxu).

*Research* (`/high` `/pubs`)

My research interests broadly span compiler systems, operating systems, and systems software, especially language virtual machines, optimizing compilers, and Android systems. I'm now working on leveraging large language models to facilitate compiler optimization and fuzzing.

**Keywords**: compilers (LLVM/GCC), language VMs (HotSpot/OpenJ9/Graal/ART; V8/JSC), Android systems, LLM agents
