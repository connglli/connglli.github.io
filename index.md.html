**Cong Li (李聪)** Ph.D student @ Nanjing University Visiting scholar @ ETH Zurich congli@smail.nju.edu.cn ![ ](PHOTO.jpeg width="25%") Cong Li is a fifth-year Ph.D student in the [Institute of Computer Software (ICS)](https://cs.nju.edu.cn/ics) at Nanjing University (NJU), advised by [Dr. Yanyan Jiang](https://cs.nju.edu.cn/ics/people/yanyanjiang/index.html) and [Prof. Chang Xu](https://cs.nju.edu.cn/changxu/) He is also a joint student of the [Advanced Software Technologies (AST) Lab](https://ast.inf.ethz.ch) at ETH Zurich, co-advised by [Prof. Zhendong Su](https://people.inf.ethz.ch/suz). His research interests span software engineering and programming languages, especially compiler and virtual machine testing, program synthesis, and Android app testing. He is now working on fuzzing the JIT compilers of modern virtual machines like JVM (HotSpot, OpenJ9, etc.). Education =================================================================== Visiting
